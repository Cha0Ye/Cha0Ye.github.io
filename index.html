About Me

I am a San Francisco native. As a kid, I was always intrigued by electronics. I would rip apart electronics laying around the house just to see how things functioned. This lead me to pursue an education in Electrical/Electronics Engineering. My background is in Logic Design, essentially computer engineering, but the hardware side of things.

After college, I acquired a job as a Field Application engineer which allowed me to visit many interesting places across the country. This gave me the chance to visit many places out of California where I interacted with many of the locals and realized how beautiful and vibrant home is.

However, I was recently introduced to web development and fell in love. I feel as if I have already learned the hardware and the physics aspect of it and now shifting my curiousity towards the software applications of computers.
